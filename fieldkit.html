Glossary

Buildsheet™
Structured table used to capture prompt architecture, variables, and proof results.

Framework
A system of repeatable patterns that ensures structured reasoning and results.

Prompt Chain
A sequence of interconnected prompts forming a workflow or proof pipeline.

Proof Artifact™
Evidence-based output that validates a prompt or process against a measurable goal.
